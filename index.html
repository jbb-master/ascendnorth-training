Content & training packages

What will you learn?

We start with the fundamentals and build step by step from there. This creates a solid foundation you can safely build on – whether for sport shooting or self-defence.

  • safe handling & fundamental safety rules
  • loading, unloading and magazine management
  • stable shooting positions and grip
  • sight picture, target acquisition and trigger control
  • accurate first hits at short distance
  • step-by-step recoil control
  • basics of sport shooting
  • range etiquette and safety culture
  • calm, controlled handling without unnecessary stress
Training packages
Basic Beginner (approx. 90 minutes)
Your first safe contact with the pistol – ideal to get to know the world of shooting.
  • safety basics & range behaviour
  • safe handling & first live-fire shots
  • fundamentals of a stable shot pattern
Beginner Plus (approx. 2 hours)
For those who want to go beyond the very first steps and work on technique and safety.
  • improved stance & grip
  • sight picture, trigger work and more consistent hits
  • initial controlled shot strings at short distance
Foundation course (approx. 2–3 hours)
For beginners with some prior experience who want to deepen their technique and safety.
  • refining technique and shooting sequence
  • more efficient shot patterns & repeatability
  • first basic movement and position changes

Frequently asked questions (FAQ)
I have never shot before – is that a problem?

Not at all. My trainings are specifically designed for people with little or no firearms experience. We start with the basics and build up step by step.

What equipment do I need?

If you do not yet own a firearm, I provide the pistol and the necessary safety equipment. If you already have your own pistol, we can of course train with your firearm instead.

How safe is the training?

Safety always comes first. All training is conducted under clear safety rules and every step is supervised directly. If at any point I feel that something is unsafe, we stop, talk about it and adjust.

Where does the training take place?

All trainings take place exclusively at officially approved shooting ranges.

Is there a minimum age?

When accompanied by a legal guardian, an introduction to shooting sports is possible from the age of 12. The minimum age for training without a legal guardian present is 16 years.

Which pistols can I train with?

If you do not yet own your own firearm, we will typically train with a Glock 17, as in my opinion it is an excellent choice for entering the world of sport shooting and/or self-defence. It is very safe, extremely reliable and easy to operate. We can also work with a SIG P365XL or a Heckler & Koch USP 45 if suitable for your goals and experience.
